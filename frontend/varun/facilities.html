College Facilities

Library

The library has a wide collection of books, journals, and electronic resources to support students' learning and research needs.

Computer Lab

The computer lab is equipped with the latest hardware and software to provide students with the tools and skills they need for their coursework and projects.

Science Labs

The science labs are fully equipped with state-of-the-art equipment and materials to support students' learning and experimentation in various disciplines of science.

Sports Complex

The sports complex includes a gymnasium, swimming pool, tennis courts, and other facilities to support students' physical fitness and recreational needs.

Cafeteria

The cafeteria offers a variety of food and beverages to cater to students' dietary preferences and needs.

Student Center

The student center provides a comfortable and conducive space for students to socialize, study, and relax.

Parking Lot

The parking lot offers ample space for students' vehicles, ensuring safe and convenient transportation to and from campus.

Health Center

The health center provides medical and counseling services to students, ensuring their physical and mental well-being.

Counseling Services

The counseling services provide guidance and support to students, helping them to overcome personal and academic challenges and achieve their goals.
